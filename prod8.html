MSI Radeon RX 6650 XT

The MSI Radeon RX 6650 XT is a high-performance graphics card designed for gaming and content creation. It features 8GB of GDDR6 memory, a boost clock speed of up to 2035 MHz, and advanced cooling technology for improved performance and longevity.

Price: $329.99

Description

The MSI Radeon RX 6650 XT is a powerful graphics card designed to deliver exceptional gaming and content creation performance. With 8GB of GDDR6 memory and a boost clock speed of up to 2035 MHz, the 6650 XT is capable of handling demanding games and applications with ease. The graphics card features advanced cooling technology, including MSI's Torx Fan 4.0 design, which features two unique blade designs that work together to improve airflow and reduce noise levels. The card also features a precision-machined heatsink that improves thermal performance and improves longevity.

The 6650 XT is built on AMD's advanced 7nm manufacturing process, which ensures energy-efficient power management while delivering high clock speeds and low-latency performance. The graphics card also supports advanced technologies, such as DirectX 12 Ultimate, Vulkan, and Radeon Anti-Lag, which improves input-to-display response time and reduces input lag. The 6650 XT is compatible with a wide range of motherboards and features PCIe 4.0 support, which provides twice the bandwidth of PCIe 3.0, allowing for faster data transfer rates between the graphics card and other components.
