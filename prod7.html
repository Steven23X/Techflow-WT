Corsair TX550M

The Corsair TX550M is a semi-modular power supply unit designed for high-performance desktop computers. It features a 80 Plus Gold certification, a 550-watt power output, and a semi-modular design that allows for easy cable management and customization.

Price: $65.99

Description

The Corsair TX550M is a high-performance power supply unit (PSU) designed to provide reliable power to desktop computers. With a 550-watt power output, the TX550M is capable of powering high-performance graphics cards and other components required for demanding applications, such as gaming and content creation. The TX550M features an 80 Plus Gold certification, which means it operates at high energy efficiency, resulting in lower energy costs and reduced environmental impact. The PSU also features a semi-modular design, which allows users to connect only the cables they need, reducing clutter and improving airflow for better cooling performance. The modular cables are also flat and easy to bend, allowing for easier cable routing and management.

The PSU features a quiet 120mm fan that operates in silent mode at low to moderate loads, reducing noise levels and improving acoustic performance. The fan speed is also adjustable using the Corsair Link software, allowing users to customize fan speed and noise levels based on their preferences. The TX550M also features advanced protection mechanisms, including over-voltage, under-voltage, over-current, and short-circuit protection, which ensures that the PSU and connected components are protected against electrical damage.
