Description

The Corsair iCUE H150i is a premium liquid CPU cooler designed for high-performance gaming and enthusiast systems. Featuring a 360mm radiator and three 120mm ML Series magnetic levitation PWM fans, the cooler delivers efficient and effective cooling performance for demanding CPUs. The iCUE H150i also features customizable RGB lighting, allowing users to personalize the cooler's look and integrate it into their existing RGB lighting system. The cooler's lighting can be easily customized and synchronized with other Corsair RGB products using the company's iCUE software.

The iCUE H150i's software also offers precise control over the cooler's performance and noise levels. Users can adjust fan speeds, monitor temperatures, and customize fan curves to optimize the cooler's performance for their specific system and usage scenario. Additionally, the iCUE H150i's pump and cold plate are designed for maximum cooling efficiency, featuring a low-profile design for better compatibility with a wide range of systems. The cooler also comes equipped with a high-performance thermal compound, ensuring optimal heat transfer from the CPU to the cooler.
