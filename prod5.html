Description

The MSI MAG VAMPIRIC 100R is a mid-tower gaming PC case that offers a perfect balance of style and performance. The case features a sleek, modern design with a tempered glass side panel that allows users to showcase their system's components and RGB lighting. The case provides ample space for high-end gaming components, including large graphics cards up to 350mm in length and multiple storage drives, with support for up to 2x 3.5" HDDs and 4x 2.5" SSDs. It also features a tool-free design, making installation and maintenance a breeze.

The VAMPIRIC 100R also includes multiple cooling options, with support for up to six fans and a 240mm radiator in the front panel. The case also includes dust filters on the top, front, and bottom panels for improved airflow and easy maintenance. The case's RGB lighting is fully customizable, with support for MSI's Mystic Light software, which allows users to control the lighting effects and synchronize them with other Mystic Light-compatible components.
